Roasted Liriope roots

Liriope is a plant with pretty purple flowers and its roots are eaten. The root is widely used as a medicinal herb in

oriental medicine, but is also used at home.

Roasted Liriope is 100% produced in Cheongyang, South Chungcheong Province.

Please note before purchasing

Dried and roasted Liriope have completely different colors.

Roasted Liriope Intake TIP

Wash 20-30g of roasted Liriope lightly under running water,

then add it to 2L of lukewarm water, steep it, and drink it.

If you drink it like water throughout the day, you can see good effects on your bronchi.

Cooking TIP

It is good to use when making soup dishes.

Samgyetang, noodle soup, stew, etc.
